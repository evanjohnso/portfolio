A bit about me...

I'm a web and mobile app developer based out of Portland, Oregon. I mostly develop with JavaScript frameworks such as Angular2, React, and React Native, but always find myself experimenting with new tools and languages to see how they compare. Plus, I love a good bash session configuring and installing new dependencies in Linux. Professionally, my goal is to join a team of developers passionate about building a secure, scalable, and sane code base.

On a personal note, I strive towards a balance of two worlds. I'm an extroverted nerd who's obsessed with skiing and camping, yet spend most of my time inside writing code. Prior to starting my career in tech, I lived on four continents and visited nearly 30 countries. I had altitude sickness at 17,000 feet in Nepal and rode a motorcycle clear across Vietnam for 9 weeks. I'm a volunteer in my local community and an active member in the Portland tech scene. I care deeply about how technology is shaping our future, and aim to be a positive voice in the conversation.
