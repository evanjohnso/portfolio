A byte about me...

I am a web and mobile app developer based in Portland, Oregon. I develop primarily with JavaScript frameworks such as Angular2 and React. I also have experience in Java, Android, React Native, Python, and C. I always enjoy experimenting with new tools and languages to see how they compare. Plus, I love a good bash session configuring and installing new dependencies in Linux. My professional goal is to join a team of developers passionate about building a secure, scalable, and elegant code base.

Personally I aim toward a balanced life. I am a "nerdy extrovert" passionate about computer science and efficient algorithms. On the other side of the life equation, I love skiing, camping, and traveling (most recently a nine week motorcycle tour of Vietnam and a three week trek in the Nepalese Himalaya). I am an active member of the Portland tech scene and volunteer in the community.
